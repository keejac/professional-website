Techniques to Writing a Competitive Grant

On-Demand Course 
Harvard Catalyst

Product Summary

This course teaches participants foundational techniques to writing a competitive grant proposal for clinical and translational research projects agnostic of funding body. The course seeds other funding courses that discuss aspects of applying for funds from the NIH, non-NIH government agencies, industry, and foundations and philanthropies.

My Impact

  • Transformed format from cohort-bound to on-demand; shipped in 2024
  • Developed and implemented user interactives, including responsive grant applications and hotspot work plans
  • Developed and implemented lo-fi take-away course materials and worksheets for offline learning
  • Improved user retention by 280+% over similar, previously offered version of course

Genomic Medicine in Clinical Practice

On-Demand Course
Harvard Catalyst

Product Summary

This course explores the role of genomic medicine in clinical care, providing health care providers with a technical foundation and recommending practical opportunities for integrating genetic testing in clinical work with patients and research participants.

My Impact

  • Shipped product as a cohort-bound offering in 2022 and 2023
  • Transformed format from cohort-bound to on-demand; shipped in 2024
  • Piloted modular design that allows learners to enroll in sections instead of whole course
  • Rated highest learner satisfaction among online offerings in 2024 (+1.4 standard deviations above average)

Values-Based Metrics Development

Harvard Catalyst

Project Summary

Worked with leadership and peers to create a dashboard that shows key metrics based on our values. We started by discussing what mattered most to each person, then connected those ideas to metrics we could measure. I collaborated with our team's web specialist to design and connect a metrics dashboard to live products so the team could instantly see how we were doing. This tool made it easier to stay aligned and track progress together.
